ARES

A picture of us working on our new project!

We are an upstart programming company formed by four friends who love ICT. The letters in ARES were formed by the first letter of our first names: Arianne, Reever, Eaden, and Stephanie. Our company's name is also a reference to the mythological Greek god who represents war and courage. Coding takes courage and determination because it is like a war between you and the many possible errors. However, thanks to the combined abilities of each member of this group, we are able to rise above these problems and reign victorious.

A

A is for Arianne Aguilar; Arianne has visions of how the webpage should look and work. By choosing the concepts and the themes, the foundation of our webpage is provided and it gives everyone one clear destination to walk towards.

R

R is for Reever Casimiro. Reever is the soul of the group. He works in the background, creating designs that ultimately adds the cherry on top to our website. Our site is prettier thanks to Reever.

E

E is for Eaden Bulo. Eaden has a love-hate relationship with coding. He may crashout because of it, but he is still the most enthusiastic and motivated one in our team. He even codes in his sleep. His drive to finish a webpage speeds up our journey to success!

S

S is for Stephanie Flores; Steph thinks in binary code, and she might even be a cyborg. She is the problem-solver of the team because in every error we encounter, she's the one who has a solution. In every code she adds, our page becomes more magical.
